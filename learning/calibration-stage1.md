## Findings & threshold decisions (re-append after every `calibrate` regen)

Corpus has grown to 20 graded pieces (19 positive-tier with stills, 9 with
multi-window clips). Hard gates `no_blowout` + `dominant_hues` hold at
19/19. Per-piece-aggregate rule for `squint_macro` (>=75% of core stills,
dual-level x dual-polarity) holds at 18/19.

**2026-06-20 re-fits (kinetic-energy-v5 filed triggers):**
- `warp_err_max` 0.12 -> 0.60. The old value (fitted on n=25 clips / 4
  pieces before any always-on sub-beat-shimmer piece) false-failed 6 of 9
  current positives тАФ warp_err now spans p50 0.065 / p90 0.23 / max 0.54
  (surfin-at-mazatlan, a ship-it). warp_err is the COARSE catastrophic-noise
  guard (pure noise -> normalized warp ~1.0); the fine trackability signal is
  jerk_smooth (0.30) + pursuit speed (30 deg/s). Result: trackability_all
  6/9-ish -> **9/9**, no negative-tier leak (negatives have no clips).
- `cursor_bounded` re-fit to a NULL-PAIR baseline (mirrors the stills' drift
  pair). The proxy compares two independent runs of a chaotic state-bearing
  sim, which decorrelate toward ~2.0 even with no cursor; the old absolute
  0.30 bar was meaningless. Now: capture a second music-only run
  (`matrix-music-b`), and pass iff hold-vs-music delta <= 2x the
  music-vs-music(null) delta. On kinetic-energy: signal 1.19 < null 1.42 <
  bar 2.84 -> pass (the held cursor changes the field LESS than re-running the
  music does). Both v5 documented metric overrides are now genuine passes.

**Still provisional:** `window_divergence` (7/9, n=9) and the clip metrics
generally тАФ the multi-window-clip corpus is small. `cursor_bounded`'s
null-pair calibration is validated on one piece so far; it accumulates as
more cursor pieces get the `matrix-music-b` capture.

**Corpus skew caveat stands:** one negative-tier piece with stills, zero with
clips. Positive pass-rates measure recall, not discrimination; negative
evidence accumulates via the evidence-snapshot convention.
